Schedule

The symposium will last from 10:00 to 13:30 UTC on each day, Monday, Nov. 16 to Thursday, Nov. 19, 2020. On each day, we will have two sessions of 90 minutes duration, separated by a coffee (lunch, or dinner) break. Sessions include an invited highlight talk of 25' plus 5' and contributed talks of 12' plus 3' question time.

On the last day (Thu., Nov. 19), the symposium is closed 15 minutes later.
